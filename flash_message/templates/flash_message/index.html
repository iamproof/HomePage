About the Flash Message

Using Python random library, and Django flash messages with databse.
Our orange friend show random information about content of the site, for example the posts in the blog or different polls with random answers, and also a couple of useful information about logged in or logged out users. The maximum of rows are 5, so keep pushing refresh(f5) or the title "About the Flash Message".
